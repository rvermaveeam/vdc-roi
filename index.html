Beyond the Numbers
These figures model direct, quantifiable costs only. The true value of Veeam protection extends further — and cannot be fully priced from published data.
Microsoft Shared Responsibility Gap
Microsoft protects its infrastructure, not your data. M365 backup and recovery is explicitly your responsibility under their Service Agreement.
eDiscovery & Legal Hold
Granular point-in-time restore enables rapid response to legal, HR, or regulatory discovery requests — inability to produce data on demand carries independent legal risk.
Audit & Compliance Evidence
Immutable backup logs serve as verifiable evidence for ISO 27001, SOC 2, MAS TRM, and OJK frameworks — reducing auditor scrutiny and supporting certification renewals.
Reputational & Brand Continuity
The modelled customer impact captures near-term revenue. The reputational tail — press, partner confidence, talent — extends well beyond what any model can quantify.
